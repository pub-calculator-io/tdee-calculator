Use our accurate TDEE Calculator to determine your Total Daily Energy Expenditure. Discover how many calories you burn daily to reach your weight goals faster.

[https://www.calculator.io/tdee-calculator/](https://www.calculator.io/tdee-calculator/)

== Usage ==

Add the TDEE Calculator shortcode to your page, post or sidebar:

`[calcio_tdee_calculator]`

Add the following code to your template where you would like the TDEE Calculator to appear:

`<?php display_calcio_tdee_calculator(); ?>`

== Installation ==

1. Upload the TDEE Calculator /calcio_tdee_calculator/ folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the TDEE Calculator plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress.
